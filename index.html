Our Story

25 Years of Beauty
& Trust

Anchal Cosmetics began its journey in 2000 at the heart of Barasat's AC Market. What started as a small cosmetics shop has grown into one of Barasat's most loved beauty destinations — trusted by thousands of families for genuine products, fair prices, and a shopping experience that always feels personal.

Walk in, and you'll find a warm, stocked space where every shelf tells you this isn't just a store — it's a neighbourhood institution.
